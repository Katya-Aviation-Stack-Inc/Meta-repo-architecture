### ‚ùå **Unacceptable Behavior**

- **Misinformation**: Spreading false information about aviation technologies or safety
- **Unauthorized Access**: Attempting to gain unauthorized access to systems or data
- **Intellectual Property**: Violating intellectual property rights or licenses

## ‚öñÔ∏è **Enforcement Guidelines**

### üéØ **Enforcement Principles**

#### **Consistency**
- Apply standards consistently across all community members
- Use transparent processes for decision-making
- Document all enforcement actions

#### **Fairness**
- Consider context and intent
- Provide opportunity for response
- Use proportional responses

#### **Education**
- Focus on education and improvement
- Provide clear guidance for behavior change
- Support community members in learning

### üìä **Response Levels**

#### **Level 1: Warning**
- **Applicable for**: First-time minor violations
- **Action**: Private warning with educational resources
- **Follow-up**: Monitor for improvement
- **Duration**: No formal restriction

#### **Level 2: Temporary Suspension**
- **Applicable for**: Repeated minor violations or single major violation
- **Action**: Temporary suspension from community participation
- **Duration**: 1-4 weeks, depending on severity
- **Conditions**: Educational requirements for reinstatement

#### **Level 3: Permanent Ban**
- **Applicable for**: Severe violations, repeated major violations, or threats
- **Action**: Permanent ban from all community participation
- **Appeal**: Limited appeal process available
- **Notification**: Community notified as appropriate

### üîç **Investigation Process**

#### **Initial Assessment**
1. **Acknowledge Receipt**: Confirm receipt of report within 24 hours
2. **Initial Review**: Assess severity and immediate action needed
3. **Privacy Protection**: Protect reporter confidentiality
4. **Evidence Collection**: Gather relevant information

#### **Formal Investigation**
1. **Investigation Team**: Assign appropriate team members
2. **Communication**: Contact involved parties
3. **Evidence Review**: Analyze all available evidence
4. **Decision Making**: Reach consensus on appropriate action

#### **Resolution**
1. **Action Implementation**: Apply decided consequences
2. **Notification**: Inform relevant parties
3. **Documentation**: Record resolution details
4. **Follow-up**: Monitor for compliance
